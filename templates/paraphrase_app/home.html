About This Project

This research-based NLP model specializes in identifying and classifying different types of paraphrases in Urdu text. Our system can distinguish between 14 distinct paraphrase categories including lexical, syntactic, semantic, and morphological variations.

Built using state-of-the-art transformer models and trained on a comprehensive dataset of Urdu sentence pairs, this tool provides accurate classification with confidence scoring for academic and research purposes.
